# Inferencer
Public roadmap for [Inferencer](https://inferencer.com). Ideas, feature requests, and bug reports are all welcome.

- Ask questions
- Vote for improvements
- Share your **ideas** and feature requests
- Report any **bugs** or issues you encounter
- Follow the progress of upcoming features and improvements

We value your input and want to build Inferencer together with our community.

---

## Voting

Our queue is generally sorted by:
- Highest Engagement: Issues with the most likes, reactions, and comments
- Recent Activity: Issues that have been recently updated (sort by Last Updated for visibility)

Visit the [Issues](https://github.com/inferencerlabs/inferencer-feedback/issues?q=is%3Aissue%20state%3Aopen%20sort%3Aupdated-desc) section and leave a comment such as +1, which directly bumps an issue up the queue.

Your voice matters.

---

## How to Submit Feedback or Ask Questions

1. Visit the [Issues](https://github.com/inferencerlabs/inferencer-feedback/issues?q=is%3Aissue%20state%3Aopen%20sort%3Aupdated-desc) section and check to see if your suggestion or bug has already been reported.
2. If not, create a new issue with a prefix, e.g.:
   - **[Feature]** – Suggest new ideas or improvements  
   - **[Bug]** – Report any problems or unexpected results
   - **[Question]** – Ask any questions

Please be as detailed as possible so we can understand your issue.

---

## Contribution Guidelines

We welcome all constructive feedback and ideas.

Please:
- Be respectful and clear  
- Avoid duplicate reports by searching existing issues  
- Provide as much context as possible
- Avoid direct links to or criticism of other communities

---

## Need Help or Have Questions?

Feel free to reach out by opening an issue or contacting us at [Inferencer](https://inferencer.com).
